Hello!

I'm Janet Delavan, an Illustrator and Graphic Designer based in New York City. View some of my work below.

Janet Delavan is a self-taught illustrator and communication design undergrad at Parsons School of Design in NYC. With a passion for learning new things, and discovering new interests, her style is constantly evolving. Her Illustrations focus on joyful compositions and warm color palettes to make life a little brighter. They believe in approaching every project with mindfulness and determination to make each creation better than the last. When she isn't designing, she spends her free time playing tennis, getting late-night coffee, and spending time with her friends!
